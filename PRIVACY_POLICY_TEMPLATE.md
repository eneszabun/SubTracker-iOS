# Privacy Policy for SubTracker

**Last Updated:** 17 Ocak 2026

## Introduction

SubTracker ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the SubTracker mobile application (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information

When you use SubTracker, we collect the following information:

- **Apple Sign In Information:** Your name and email address provided through Apple Sign In
- **Subscription Data:** Information about your subscriptions that you manually enter into the app, including:
  - Subscription names
  - Costs and currency
  - Renewal dates
  - Categories
  - Notes

### 2. Device Information

We do NOT collect any device-specific information, usage analytics, or tracking data.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

- Provide and maintain the App's functionality
- Send you renewal notifications for your subscriptions (if you grant notification permissions)
- Sync your data across your devices using iCloud (if you enable this feature and have a Pro subscription)
- Provide customer support

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage

All your subscription data is stored locally on your device using Core Data. We do NOT have access to this data.

### iCloud Storage

If you enable iCloud synchronization (Pro feature), your subscription data is stored in your personal iCloud account. This data is:

- Encrypted by Apple
- Accessible only to you
- NOT accessible to SubTracker developers
- Governed by Apple's Privacy Policy

## Third-Party Services

### Apple Services

SubTracker uses the following Apple services:

1. **Apple Sign In:** For user authentication
2. **iCloud:** For optional data synchronization (Pro feature)
3. **StoreKit:** For in-app purchases
4. **UserNotifications:** For renewal reminders
5. **Spotlight:** For system-wide search integration
6. **Siri & Shortcuts:** For voice commands

These services are governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

### Analytics and Tracking

**We do NOT use any third-party analytics or tracking services.** SubTracker does NOT contain:

- Google Analytics
- Facebook SDK
- Firebase Analytics
- Crashlytics
- Any advertising SDKs
- Any behavioral tracking

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

### Access Your Data

All your data is stored locally on your device. You can view, edit, or export your subscription data directly from the App.

### Delete Your Data

You can delete:

- Individual subscriptions within the App
- All your data by deleting the App from your device

### Opt-Out of Notifications

You can disable notifications at any time through:

- iOS Settings → SubTracker → Notifications
- SubTracker App → Settings → Notifications

## Children's Privacy

SubTracker is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

- Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
- Providing an in-app notification

## Data Retention

Your subscription data is retained:

- **Locally:** Until you delete the app or manually delete subscriptions
- **iCloud:** According to your iCloud storage settings and retention policies

## International Data Transfers

Since all data is stored locally on your device or in your iCloud account, there are no international data transfers by SubTracker.

## GDPR Compliance (For EU Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under GDPR:

- **Right to Access:** Request a copy of your data
- **Right to Rectification:** Correct inaccurate data
- **Right to Erasure:** Request deletion of your data
- **Right to Data Portability:** Export your data

Since all data is stored locally, you can exercise these rights directly through the App.
